Case Study

The Morrison Kitchen Transformation

A complete gut renovation of a 1995 colonial kitchen — transformed into a bright, modern transitional space the whole family loves.

Project Overview

The Morrison family had lived with a cramped, dated kitchen for 12 years. Their vision: an open, light-filled kitchen that could handle family life and serious entertaining. We completely restructured the layout — removing a load-bearing wall, relocating the island, and tripling the window area.

The result is a 440 sq ft kitchen that feels twice the size, centers on a 10-foot waterfall island, and features a full-height butler's pantry — completed in 7 weeks, on-budget.

Project Details

📐Project Size440 sq ft
📅Duration7 weeks
💰Investment Range$85,000 – $95,000
📍LocationWestfield, NJ
🏠Home Type1995 Colonial

Scope of Work

Structural wall removal & beam installation
Full plumbing & electrical relocation
Custom inset cabinetry in warm white
Calacatta quartz waterfall island countertop
Handcrafted subway tile backsplash (floor-to-ceiling)
10-foot central island with seating for 5
Wolf range & Sub-Zero refrigerator integration
Hardwood floor installation & refinishing
Butler's pantry with wine storage
Custom hood design & installation
Under-cabinet LED lighting system
Pot filler & prep sink addition

Meet the Founder
David Reeves
Founder & Master Craftsman

Growing up in a family of tradesmen, David learned early that a home is more than walls and countertops — it's the stage for a family's most important moments. After earning his construction management degree and spending 8 years as a lead carpenter for a luxury custom home builder, he founded 2nd Avenue Design in 2008 with a simple mission: bring design-build quality to everyday homeowners.

David personally oversees every project and remains hands-on in the field — something he believes separates 2nd Avenue from larger, less personal firms. His dual expertise in both design and construction means clients never have to choose between a beautiful vision and practical execution.
